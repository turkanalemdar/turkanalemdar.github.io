UX Methods

User Research

I believe engaging with the target audience early and often is crucial to success. To identify the actual needs of the potential users, I conduct user interviews, surveys and card-sorting.

Wireframing/Prototyping

From two-dimensional illustration of a page interface to clickable and interactive prototypes! Using Figma/Adobe XD I create clickable protoypes to illustrate the viability and usability of the product or feature.

Information Architecture

By designing the information space for the product or service, I break tasks down into tiny steps so that I can identify important decisions and potential points of friction.

Competitive Analysis

Identifying underserved opportunities in the market, and spotting weaknesses in a competitor‚Äôs user experience would provide many angles for both developing a user friendly product and for using these angles to the advantage to outperform them.

User Personas

By bringing the actual users' perspectives to the forefront, I hypothesize clear actions the users might take while using the product or service.

Usability Testing

To evaluate if a prototype or experience meets its intended purpose, I perform usability testings and report the results.
