UX Methods

User Research

I believe engaging with the target audience early and often is crucial to success. To identify the actual needs of the potential users, I conduct user interviews, surveys and card-sorting.

Wireframing/Prototyping

From two-dimensional illustration of a page interface to clickable and interactive prototypes! Using Figma/Adone XD I create clickable protoypes to illustrate the viability and usability of the product or feature.

Information Architecture

By designing the information space for the product or service, I break tasks down into tiny steps so that I can identify important decisions and potential points of friction.
